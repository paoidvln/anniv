How It All Began

It started simply a message, a conversation that stretched longer than either of us expected. We talked about ordinary things and somehow, between the lines, something quiet began to take shape.

Distance was always there. But you had a way of making the miles feel smaller. Your words arrived and I felt less alone in whatever corner of the world I was in.

The beginning

The first message. Neither of us knew it was the start of something we'd still be talking about a year later.

First real conversations

Late nights, different time zones, bad signal sometimes — yet we stayed. That persistence meant something.

Learning each other

The fears, the soft spots, the small joys. I learned the shape of you through words and time zones.

One year in

And here we are. Still here. Still trying. Still choosing each other despite everything.

My love, Liezel

Gusto ko lang mag-thank you sa'yo, hindi lang basta thank you, kundi yung pasasalamat na galing talaga sa heart ko. Salamat sa pag-stay. Salamat sa pagpili sa'kin, kahit alam kong hindi naging madali.

Alam ko na dumaan tayo sa mga panahong sobrang hirap—yung tipong pwede ka namang sumuko, pwede mong piliin na lang na iwan lahat. Pero hindi mo ginawa. Nanatili ka. Kahit pagod ka na minsan, kahit nasasaktan ka na, pinili mo pa rin ako. At dun ko mas nare-realize kung gaano kita kamahal kasi hindi lahat ng tao kayang gawin yung ginagawa mo.

Pasensya na kung may mga pagkakataon na ako pa yung nagiging dahilan ng pagod mo. Hindi ko man agad naayos lahat, pero gusto kong malaman mo na nakikita ko lahat ng effort mo, lahat ng sacrifices mo, at hindi ko yun tine-take for granted. Hindi ka nag-stay sa madaling part, pero nag-stay ka sa mga araw na magulo, sa mga moments na hindi tayo okay, sa mga oras na kailangan pa nating ayusin ang isa’t isa.At sobrang thankful ako doon.

Kung may isang bagay akong gustong ipangako, yun ay gagawin ko yung best ko para hindi masayang yung pag-stay mo. Gusto kong maging worth it yung lahat ng tiniis mo para sa'kin, para sa’tin. Kasi hindi ko kayang isipin na mawala ka.

Thank you for choosing me, kahit hindi ako perfect.

Thank you for loving me, kahit minsan mahirap ako mahalin.

Mahal na mahal kita, mahal, Baby Love

always yours, Paul♡
